Eras

Nine years of research grouped into five distinct eras, each with its own character and breakthrough themes.

Genesis

Sep 2017 – Dec 2017

The forum launches. Early discussions lay groundwork for Casper proof-of-stake, stateless client design, and initial sharding concepts.

Byzantium

Scaling Wars

2018

An explosion of competing scaling proposals: Plasma variants, sharding execution models, VDFs, and fierce Casper FFG vs CBC debates. The year of maximum research divergence.

Constantinople

Eth2 Design

2019 – 2020

Convergence on a concrete design. Phase 0 and Phase 1 specs crystallize. The beacon chain launches. Rollups emerge as the dominant scaling paradigm, reshaping the roadmap.

Istanbul Phase 0

Post-Merge Build

2021 – 2022

The PoW-to-PoS Merge executes. EIP-1559 goes live. PBS and MEV awareness surge. Research shifts from "how to build PoS" to "how to build on PoS."

Berlin London Altair The Merge

Endgame Architecture

2023 – 2026

Attention turns to the protocol's endgame: enshrined PBS, single-slot finality, blob scaling with PeerDAS, based rollups, and sustainable staking economics.

Methodology

How the map is built: scraping, analysis, and visualization.

1

Data Collection

2,903 topics are scraped from ethresear.ch, capturing titles, authors, dates, post content, tags, categories, view counts, likes, and reply counts.

2

Citation Graph

Cross-references between topics are extracted from post links, building a directed citation graph. Internal links (outgoing) and reflection links (incoming) are tracked.

3

Thread Classification

Topics are assigned to research threads using title pattern matching, tag analysis, author signals, and category hints. Each thread represents a sustained line of inquiry.

4

Influence Scoring

Each topic gets an influence score combining in-degree (30%), likes (25%), log-views (20%), discussion depth (15%), and author prolificness (10%).

5

EIP Mapping

EIP references are extracted from topic titles and post text, linking research discussions to concrete protocol proposals and shipped fork upgrades.

6

Visualization

The analysis feeds into an interactive D3.js visualization with three views: timeline swim lanes, citation network, and co-author network.
